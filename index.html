For Club Admins

The best tool for
running your club.

Stop herding members through Facebook groups and text threads. DiscGolfRankings gives you a real command center for your club — right from your phone.

Message All Members — One Button

Reach your entire club instantly. Announcements, weather cancellations, event reminders — sent to everyone with a single tap.

Shareable Sign-Up Links

Post a link anywhere — Facebook, your website, a flyer — and new members join in seconds. No paperwork, no manual roster updates.

Digital Bag Tags — No More Plastic

Assign, transfer, and manage bag tag numbers entirely in the app. Members always have their tag. You never have to reprint one again.

Full Member Roster View

See who's in your club, their bag tag numbers, rankings, and activity — all in a clean dashboard built for admin work.

Club Leaderboard Management

Rankings update automatically. No spreadsheets, no manual entry. Your club standings are always live and accurate.

Player-Confirmed Scores — No Disputes

Every group round needs sign-off from each player before tags move. You stop refereeing arguments — the app settles it.

Events & Challenges

Create events, manage entries, and track results. Keep your members engaged and competing all season long.
